# Project Proposal Instructions

This document outlines what is required for the course project proposal.

---

## Overview

The proposal is the first step of the course project. You will pick a dataset and two research questions, then apply deep learning methods to answer them. The final project will include both a written report and an oral presentation.

---

## Step 1: Frame the Problem

You need two research questions, one of each type below.

**Classification**
Predicting a category or class. Example: Can we predict whether an employee will leave a company based on their salary and years of experience?

**Regression**
Predicting a continuous value. Example: Can I predict a students final exam score based on attendance rate and hours studying per week?

A good research question should be specific, measurable, and answerable with the right data. Avoid broad questions like "How does X affect people?" or "What trends are there in X?"

---

## Step 1 Questions to Answer

- How would the answers to your RQs be useful or beneficial to other people?
- For the classification RQ, are you more interested in precision, recall, or F1 score?
- For the classification RQ, propose two models and one baseline model you plan to compare.
- For the regression RQ, propose two models and one baseline model you plan to compare.
- For both RQs, name at least 3 major hyperparameters you plan to tune.

**Allowed non-baseline models:** neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, attention-based models, GANs, autoencoders, diffusion models

**Allowed baseline models:** logistic/linear regression, SVMs, decision trees, random forests, perceptrons

> Note: If you are a beginner, stick to basic neural networks for now. The more advanced models are covered later in the semester.

## Dataset Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Rows | 750 or more |
| Features | 10 or more |
| Format | CSV, JSON, images, or text only |
| Data types | Numerical, ordinal, categorical, or a mix |

> Image and text datasets are allowed but are harder and restricted to classification only.
> Vetoed formats: reinforcement learning, chatbots, video, 3D shapes.

---

## Step 2 Questions to Answer

- Where did you get the dataset? Include the source name and a URL.
- Does the dataset have missing data?
- What types of data are in the dataset? (numerical, categorical, tabular, image, text, etc.)
- How many rows and columns does the dataset have?
- How does this dataset help you answer your two research questions?
